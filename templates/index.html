📝 Steps

1. Login and Initial Assessment 🔐

Begin by logging into your WeCare account to access your personalized mental health dashboard. You will be asked a series of scientifically designed close-ended questions. These help assess your emotional well-being and provide a baseline for deeper analysis.

2. Open-Ended Questions 🗣️

Share your thoughts, emotions, and concerns through open-ended questions. This step allows you to express yourself freely, giving a more in-depth view of your mental health. Your responses help in generating personalized insights and tailored recommendations.

3. Feedback and Main Portal 💡

Receive an in-depth analysis of your responses, highlighting key areas of concern. Gain access to WeCare’s support features, including AI-powered chatbot assistance. Explore additional resources, self-help tools, and professional guidance tailored to your needs.
